“Snapshot: Investigative Interviews on Chinese Contemporary Museums”

Book | May 2015 - Aug 2017

• Spent 2.5 years investigating 40 non-profit contemporary private museums, art foundations, and art centers in China; interviewing 54 museum directors, founders, and CEOs, and compiled into a book over 450 pages in length. Compiled and recorded data/primary sources on private contemporary museums in sectors of museum operations, collection legality issues, corporate structure, export/ import artwork taxes, future developments, etc. • First to collect quantitative data on private contemporary art organizations in China. Project received ¥190,000 in combined sponsorship from Asia Art Archive, New Century Art Foundation, SAIC, Chao Art Center, and Shanghai Glass Museum. • Conducted a ‘TED talk’ on the project at Columbia Global Center. Book is featured in 5 major news organizations. (Artnet, Leap Magazine, Artron, Ifeng, Artyoo,) Book has sold over 300 copies in the first week, profiting over ¥60,000.
