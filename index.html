Publications

  • "Snapshot: Investigative Interviews on Chinese Contemporary Private Museums"

    05.2015 - 08.2017

    Spent 2.5 years investigating 40 contemporary private museums, art foundations, and art centers in China; 54 museum directors, founders, and CEOs, complied into a book over 450 pages in length. Compiled and recorded data/primary sources on private contemporary museums in sectors of museum operations, collection legality issues, corporate structure, export/import artwork taxes, future developments, etc. First to collect quantitative data on private contemporary art organizations in China. Project received ¥190,000 in sponsorship from Asia Art Archive, New Century Art Foundation, SAIC, Chao Art Center, and Shanghai Glass Museum. Conducted a ‘TED talk’ on the project at Columbia Global Center. Book is featured and reviewed by various major news publications. (Artnet, Leap Magazine, Artron, Ifeng, Artyoo,) Book sold over 300 copies in the first week.
